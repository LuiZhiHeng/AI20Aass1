  1. PIANO

    Studies show that playing the piano improves mental health. People who play the piano tend to experience less anxiety and depression than their nonmusical counterparts. Playing for a few minutes a day can improve self-esteem, make you feel more positive, and can lower your blood pressure. Playing the piano also makes your hands and arm muscles much stronger than the average person.
